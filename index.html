How we choose our picks

We don’t test every projector ourselves, instead, we aggregate long-term owner feedback from forums, verified Amazon reviews, and published expert evaluations from reputable tech outlets. The key questions are always: does the image hold up after 500 hours? Does the built-in battery degrade quickly? Do the smart features stay responsive after software updates? Models that score well on all three fronts make our shortlist. We also watch for common failure points: fan noise that worsens over time, HDMI handshake issues, and misleading lumen ratings. A projector may look great in a spec sheet but fall apart in real-world living rooms. Our picks are the ones that consistently earn praise for reliability, not just marketing promise.
